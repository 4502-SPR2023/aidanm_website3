Top 7 Punk Albums

This list is some of my favorite Punk albums of all time,which largely consists of albums I feel as though they pioneered sub-genres as well as Punk itself

Knife Man-AJJ

One of my favorite folk punk albums, this album holds a speacial place in my heart as it reminds me of good times when I was growing up.

Bad Brains- Self Titled

This was one of the first albums I ever bought a physical copy of, they are one of my favorites as they revolutionized the D.C punk scene.

BAD BRAINS IMAGE

Black Flag-Damaged

Another classic punk album that revolutionized the genre, one of my favorite bands ever.

MK Ultra Support Group-MSG 1

A punk album from recent times focusing on the internet. This is a complitation of a bunch of different artist, and while the album is supposed to be ironic, I do still like the music.

Bikini Kill-Yeah Yeah Yeah

I am a big fan of Bikini Kill, and the role it had they had in diversifying the genre of punk is not to be understated.

Descendants-Milo Goes to College

This was the first punk album I ever listened to, and due to this it will always be near the top of my list

Minor Threat- Self titled

This is by far my favororite album of all time, it has all of the things that make punk what it is today, with the rawness of it being a straight edge band.

My Favorite Restaurants in St. Louis

My hometown of St. Louis has a lot of really good options for restaurants, with an amazing variety of food from all over the world. While some of these restaurants are chains, I will do my best to avoid them.

Seoul Taco

6665 Delmar Blvd, St. Louis, MO 63130
(314) 863-1148
seoultaco.com

Seoul Taco is one of my favorite restaurants in the Loop and the perfect place to sit down and eat after a long day of shopping.

Gingham's Homestyle Restaurant

1566 Country Club Plaza Dr, St. Charles, MO 63303
(636) 946-0266
ginghamsrestaurant.com

Gingham's is the last diner of its kind in the St. Louis area, as it is open 24/7, and has some of the best food of its kind in the area. With it being so close to Main Street and the Ameristar, it is a perfect place to wind down after a night out.

Salt + Smoke

501 S Main St, St Charles, MO 63301
(314) 727-0200
saltandsmokebbq.com

Another great spot near Main St, serving some of the most amazing barbeque I have ever had, a great place to go for a date night (not the first as it can get a little messy).
